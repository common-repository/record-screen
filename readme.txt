Screen Recorder allows to record and play back user activity on your blog.

== Description ==

Screen Recorder is an easy way to find out what users behave on your blog. This can be useful to discover what posts and pages they visit and how they behave on these posts.
You can playback all actions user do on your site. Very useful to know what errors they make, discover their habits, record their illegal activity on your site.


== Installation ==

* activate the plugin
* Fo to the post/page you want to supervise and activate the "Record" option in meta box
* you can set global supervision on all pages/posts of your blog in settings

 
== Frequently Asked Questions ==
 
= Where are records stored  =
 
They are stored in your WP database

= Should I record all posts and pages ? =

If you have high traffic, you should avoid doing that because of bandwidth and quantity of recorded data
 
= Can I recorded global activity of an user ? =
 
No, you just record 1 post or 1 page activity

= Do I get some extra info avout user ? =

No, you just have his IP address.
